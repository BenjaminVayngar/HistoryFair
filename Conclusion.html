Conclusion

The Black Power Salute represented the fight for the right to live free from racial oppression for the African-American community. The Black Power Salute was about seeking equality, empowering, and achieving justice for communities that were faced with poverty, police violence, and discrimination. The athletes used the podium and their talents in order to give a voice to those who lacked the opportunity to speak out.
