Conclusion

The Black Power Salute carried the responsibility of advocating for the liberation of the African-American community and fighting for the right to live free from racial oppression. The Black Power Salute was about seeking equality, empowering, and achieving justice for communities that were faced with poverty, police violence, and lack of civil rights. The athletes used their platform and talent because it was necessary to give a voice to those who lacked the opportunity to speak out.
